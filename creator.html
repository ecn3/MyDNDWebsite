Character Creater Overview

There is a lot of information that player needs to go over while creating a character. A player needs to select thier race, class, background, starting equipment, starting stats, and starting skills. In addition to that depending on the race and class the player may need to choose starting spells, starting feature, subrace, and subclass or variant. With all of this information the Players Handbook offers an easy to follow guide to character creation. This goes as follows:

  1. Select your race/subrace
  2. Select your class/subclass
  3. Assign your stats
  4. Choose your skills
  5. Choose your equipment
  6. Choose spells if applicable
  7. Select your background
  8. Select your feature if applicable

While this seems logical there are problems with racial bonus not doing anything and skill crossover. For example the Dragonborn race has bonuses to Strength and Charisma while the wizard class uses only Intelligence. In Dungeons and Dragons there is no additive effect for having the same skill twice, which means if the background you selected comes with a skill that you already selected you lose out on that skill. Because of this the preferred method of creating a character is as follows:

  1. Select your class
  2. Assign your stats
  3. Select a race/subrace that benefits your class
  4. Select a background
  5. Select your feature if applicable
  6. Sellect your skills
  7. Choose your equipment
  8. Select your spells if applicable
